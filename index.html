About

I am a Belgian webdeveloper preparing for the future of the web.

On the one hand it means that I'm still in a learning phase and that's good. Everyday interesting webtechnologies evolve so I should too. On the other hand I will not deviate from best practices. Avoiding "quick & dirty" solutions is a priority.

 

This passion expresses itself in various ways.

Any website I build today is...

Responsive, making it look gorgeous on smartphones too.

Easy to use, preventing those moments of frustration.

Minimal, that's my style.

Because that is how I imagine the internet of the future.
